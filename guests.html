Robbo Williams
🪚 Sawmill Operator · QLD

Third-generation sawmiller from Far North Queensland, Robbo talks about the realities of running a portable mill business — the good seasons, the bad ones, and why he wouldn't swap it for anything.

Jess Carver

Jess pioneered her unique style of resin-and-timber wall art and turned it into a six-figure business. She breaks down the process, the failures, and how she markets her work without a gallery.

Be on the Show

Apply to Be a Guest

Hazy and Plummo are always on the hunt for interesting people from the timber world. You don't have to be famous — you just have to have a story worth telling or knowledge worth sharing.

We're Looking For

  • ✓ Woodworkers, sawmillers, carpenters, cabinet makers
  • ✓ Tool makers, timber merchants, and suppliers
  • ✓ Health professionals with trade experience
  • ✓ Women in the timber industry
  • ✓ Anyone with a genuinely ripper story
Note: The show is based in Sydney but guests can join in person or via remote recording. Being in another state or country is no barrier.
