The Story

About the Show

How two timber-loving mates and Sydneysiders turned workshop banter into one of Australia's most honest woodworking podcasts.

Our Origin

Born in the Shed, Built for the Community

The Timber Bros Podcast kicked off when Plummos and Hazy — both active creators in Australia's woodworking world — realised the conversations happening in the comments section, at markets, and between mates deserved a proper home.

"We just wanted a yarn. Turns out a lot of people wanted to listen in."

Together they represent Plummo's Timber and Hazys Wood — two well-known names in the Aussie woodworking scene on social media — bringing those same communities into a long-form, no-frills podcast format.

The show is recorded at Oz Soundz Studios in Sydney NSW, and releases monthly on all major podcast platforms.

While currently audio-only, the lads are heading to video — with full visual episodes, workshop tours, and behind-the-scenes content in the pipeline. Watch this space.

The Format
  • 🎙 Monthly episodes — new drops every four weeks
  • ⏱ 45–75 minutes — long enough to go deep, short enough to get through on the drive
  • 🎧 Audio podcast — available on Spotify, Apple, Overcast, YouTube & more

Why Listen?

What You'll Get Out of It

🪵

The Craft

Deep dives into timber species, resin techniques, finishing secrets, and the slow art of doing things right — even when it takes a little longer.

🔧

The Tools

Honest reviews, head-to-head comparisons, and no-BS advice on the gear that's worth your hard-earned. From chainsaws to chisels.

🏗️

The Business

The reality of running a timber business or workshop in the modern world — marketing, social media, pricing, and the stories behind the sawdust.

🤝

The Community

Interviews with artisans, designers, sawmillers, and people who love working with their hands. People who've earned their calluses.

❤️

Men's Health

Important conversations about mental health, physical wellbeing, and the unique pressures men in trades face — spoken plainly, without the fluff.

💜

Women's Health & Women in Timber

Celebrating and supporting the women in our industry. Health conversations that matter, and shining a light on female makers doing amazing work.
